Discover the Difference at CAA

With over 15 years of experience, Chimes Aviation Academy stands out as a leader in ab initio flying training in India. Committed to unparalleled quality and excellence, CAA offers a comprehensive training environment that nurtures every aspect of a cadet’s journey. Choosing CAA means more than just receiving top-tier flying training; it’s about becoming part of a prestigious legacy of excellence.

Flying Training Ecosystem

Our Airbases

Strategic Airbase Locations

Our airbases are strategically situated just a cross-country flight from each other, benefiting from Madhya Pradesh's favorable weather, which offers over 320 clear flying days annually. This ensures optimal flying time with minimal interruptions.

Uncontrolled Airspace

Located at state-owned government airfields with no commercial traffic, our airbases provide exclusive access to airspace dedicated to flying training, allowing for uninterrupted training sessions.

Night-Flying Capabilities

Our facilities are fully equipped for night flying, with runways fitted with PAPI lights to ensure safe and efficient landings in low visibility conditions.

Largest and Youngest Fleet

Our Fleet

CAA is proud to operate a modern fleet featuring 10 Cessna 172s, 10 Piper Archer DXs, and 2 Diamond DA42s, with plans to add more aircraft soon.

Advanced Avionics

Every aircraft is equipped with the Garmin G1000 NXi system, enhancing safety, situational awareness, and access to crucial flight data. Training on these Glass Cockpits prepares cadets for the sophisticated avionics found in commercial aircraft.

Safety Equipped

Our fleet includes advanced safety features such as Automatic Dependent Surveillance–Broadcast and Full Authority Digital Engine Control, ensuring cadets experience a high level of safety and confidence during their training.

Faculty

Experienced Instructors

Our team of instructors at CAA brings both passion for aviation and extensive teaching expertise. Rigorous evaluations guarantee that our cadets are mentored by top industry professionals.

Balanced Instructor : Cadet Ratio

CAA prioritizes personalized attention by maintaining a balanced instructor-to-cadet ratio. This approach ensures each cadet receives dedicated support and guidance throughout their training journey.

On-Campus Hostel

Our on-campus hostels at each airbase are equipped with high-quality infrastructure and focus on cadet well-being. The secure and comfortable accommodations provide modern amenities, creating a welcoming environment for our cadets.

In-house Maintenance

The in-house CAR 145 Maintenance facility, staffed by expert professionals, ensures the fleet is always airworthy. Regular interactions with the engineering staff make the students better understand the machines they fly.
