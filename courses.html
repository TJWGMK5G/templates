CAA-IndiGo Cadet Pilot Program

Start an exhilarating journey with CAA’s collaboration with IndiGo. This program offers a direct path to becoming an IndiGo pilot.

Introduction

Program Outline

Chimes Aviation Academy (CAA) is honored to partner with IndiGo, India’s largest airline, to offer the prestigious IndiGo Cadet Pilot Program.

This all-inclusive program covers everything from Ground School to Type Rating. Upon completion, cadets are fully prepared to start their careers as Junior First Officers with IndiGo.

With a focus on excellence, the IndiGo Cadet Pilot Program at CAA ensures that each cadet receives top-notch training and personalized support throughout their journey. The program is designed to meet the highest industry standards, providing students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their roles and contribute effectively to IndiGo's esteemed operations.

Duration : Full time 20-month Program
Training Modules

The Program is divided into three phases

Ground School Training

In this phase, you'll attend theory classes for all DGCA and WPC exams at our Ground School in Gurugram. The comprehensive curriculum includes:

  • Aviation Meteorology
  • Air Regulations
  • Air Navigation
  • Technical General
  • Technical specific (Single Engine)
  • Radio Telephony
  • Aviation English Language Proficiency (AELP)

Flight Training (CPL - SE/IR)

During this phase, flight training is conducted at our air bases in Dhana and Neemuch, using our state-of-the-art fleet of Cessna 172, Piper Archer DX, and Diamond DA42 aircraft. Cadets also train on DGCA-approved aircraft simulators as part of their flight training.

Modules include

  • 185 hours on all Single Engine Aircraft
  • 15 hours on Diamond DA 42 Multi-Engine Aircraft
  • 30 hours of Simulator Flying

Type Rating

After obtaining their Commercial Pilot License (CPL), cadets advance to Airbus A320 training, including Multi-Crew Coordination (MCC) and Jet Orientation Training (JOT), at a TRTO partner approved by IndiGo.

Admission

Eligibility

Education

10+2 with at least 51% in each subject (Physics, Mathematics, English) or Grade Point 6 / Grade C1 (CBSE).

For foreign qualifications, obtain an academic equivalency certificate from AIU (Association of Indian Universities).

National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) mark sheets are accepted with a minimum score of 51% in Mathematics, Physics, and English individually.

Age

Between 18 and 32 years of age

Medical

Valid Indian DGCA Class I Medical Certificate.

Passport

Valid Indian Passport

Police Verification

Criminal Record Check (CRC) Basic Disclosure Certificate.

English Language Fluency

Proficiency in both written and spoken English.

Assessment & Selection Process

Our selection process includes the following stages:

After meeting the eligibility criteria, the candidates will be called for the following states of assessment:

Stage 1: ADAPT Assessment

Candidates will undergo the ADAPT Assessment at our specialized ADAPT center. This online psychometric and aptitude test is designed to evaluate the behavioral and cognitive skills necessary for a career in aviation.

Assessment Fee: INR 30,000 (Thirty Thousand Rupees).

Stage 2: Group Task Assessment with IndiGo Panellists

In this phase, candidates participate in a Group Task Assessment to demonstrate their teamwork and problem-solving abilities.

Stage 3: Personal Interviews with IndiGo Panellists

Candidates who excel in previous stages will attend personal interviews with IndiGo Panellists. This is an opportunity to discuss their goals and experiences.

Upon successfully completing these stages, candidates will be invited to enroll and sign the Letter of Intent (LoI) with IndiGo.
