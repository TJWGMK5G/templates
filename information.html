Facility Hours

Our gym is open seven days a week, providing flexible access to accommodate your busy schedule. Enjoy early morning workouts or late-night sessions, knowing that our state-of-the-art facilities are available to you when it suits you best.

Membership Policies

We prioritize transparency in our membership policies. From cancellation terms to freeze options, we ensure that you fully understand your rights and responsibilities as a member. Our team is always available to answer any questions you may have regarding your membership.

Safety Measures

Your safety is our top priority. Titan Strength Gym implements strict hygiene protocols, regular equipment maintenance, and safety training for all staff. We also offer personal protective equipment (PPE) upon request to ensure a clean and safe workout environment.

Community Events

We believe in building a strong community around fitness. Join us for our monthly events, such as health fairs, charity workouts, and fitness challenges. These events not only promote wellness but also foster connections among our members, creating a supportive and inspiring environment.
