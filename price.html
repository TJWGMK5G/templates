Common Queries

We understand you may have some questions, so we've compiled a list to save you time.

View FAQ
What are your operating hours?

Our gym is open daily from 6 AM to 10 PM to accommodate your workout schedule. We operate seven days a week, so you can visit us at any time that suits you!

How can I register for classes?

Registering for our classes is easy! You can sign up online through our website or mobile app, as well as in person at our facility. We also offer flexible membership plans for your convenience.

Do you have instructors who can help me create a workout program?

Yes, absolutely! Our professional instructors are always ready to help you achieve your fitness goals. They can design an individualized workout program tailored to your needs and fitness level.

What is the cost of membership at your gym?

We offer various membership plans to fit your budget and needs. The cost of membership depends on the selected plan and duration of the membership term. For detailed pricing information, please contact our customer support.

What amenities do you have at the gym?

Our gym is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and high-quality facilities. Additionally, we have changing rooms, showers, relaxation areas, and water fountains for your convenience. We also offer group classes and personal training sessions for variety and maximum effectiveness in your workout.

Challenge yourself! If you love a little healthy competition, join one of our fitness challenges and prepare to push your limits.
