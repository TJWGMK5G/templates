What is your process for working with clients?

Our process begins with a meeting to discuss your needs and preferences. We then develop a design concept, provide you with preliminary sketches, and upon your approval, proceed to implement the project.

What services do you offer?

We provide a full range of interior design services, including consultations, concept development, space planning, material and furniture selection, as well as project management and decoration.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The time to complete a project depends on its scale and complexity, but on average our process takes from several weeks to several months.

Can I make changes to the project after it has started?

Yes, we are always open to changes and adjustments during the process. We aim to accommodate your needs and preferences to the fullest extent.

What design styles do you base your projects on?

We work in various styles including modern, classic, minimalistic, Scandinavian, and many others. Our priority is to create an interior that reflects your individuality and meets your needs.

How can I get in touch with your team?

You can contact us by phone, email, or through the contact form on our website. We are always happy to help you realize your interior design idea.
