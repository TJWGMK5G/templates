Our Origins

Driven by a passion for music, a dedication to craftsmanship, and a desire to create products with care, InstrumentalSound was established by Leena Labroo in 2011. Beginning in her garage, InstrumentalSound swiftly rose to prominence as a leading provider of handcrafted musical instruments in India. Named after her two daughters, Nisheeta & Aneesha, InstrumentalSound embodies the essence of familial devotion and dedication.

In 2015, Carolyn Ortiz joined InstrumentalSound, leading the expansion of our product line to encompass a diverse array of high-quality musical instruments catering to individuals, businesses, and institutions. This strategic initiative widened InstrumentalSound's market reach and cemented its reputation as a premier musical instrument brand committed to excellence.
