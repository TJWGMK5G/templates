About

At FlexZone, we're more than just a gym - we're a community dedicated to empowering individuals to lead healthier, happier lives. Our state-of-the-art facility offers a wide range of fitness options, from high-intensity workouts to mindful practices, suitable for all fitness levels and goals.

What sets us apart is our commitment to providing personalized attention and support to every member. Our experienced trainers are passionate about helping you reach your full potential, whether you're striving to lose weight, build muscle, or improve overall well-being.

But FlexZone is more than just a place to work out. It's a place where friendships are formed, goals are achieved, and obstacles are overcome. Join us at FlexZone and become part of a thriving community dedicated to fitness, health, and happiness.

The story of FlexZone Gym began in a small town where a group of fitness enthusiasts came together with one goal in mind - to create a space where everyone could find strength, health and confidence. Inspired by the idea of a community space where people could meet, develop and inspire each other, they put their heart and soul into creating FlexZone.

The first gym opened in a modest building equipped with basic exercise equipment and a simple interior. Despite the modest conditions, the atmosphere in the hall was so warm and supportive that word of it quickly spread throughout the city. Over time, FlexZone became not only a place for fitness, but also a center for fitness culture in the city. The gym expanded, adding new equipment and unique workout programs. The team of trainers was strengthened by professionals, inspired by mentors who shared their knowledge and experience with the students.

Every day FlexZone attracted more and more people who wanted to join this community of health and strength. From students to retirees, from beginners to professionals, everyone has found their place and support here. Today, FlexZone has become an integral part of the urban landscape, a symbol of healthy living and an inspiration to many. Our gym continues to grow and develop, but its soul remains the same - it is a place where everyone feels at home, where friends meet and goals become reality.

FAQs

Everything you need to know

What are your operating hours?

FlexZone is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM Monday through Saturday, and from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on Sundays.

Do you offer personal training sessions?

Yes, we offer personalized training sessions with our certified trainers. These sessions are tailored to your individual fitness goals and needs.

What types of classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of classes including HIIT, yoga, pilates, strength training, boxing, and more. Check out our class schedule for more details.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking available for our members.

Do you provide locker room facilities?

Yes, we have locker rooms equipped with showers and amenities for your convenience.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your safety is our top priority. We have implemented strict cleaning protocols, social distancing measures, and provide hand sanitizing stations throughout the facility.

How do I cancel or freeze my membership?

You can cancel or freeze your membership by contacting our front desk staff or by logging into your online account. Please refer to our membership terms and conditions for more details.
