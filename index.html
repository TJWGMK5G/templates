Your Guide to Fantasy Cricket FAQs

What is Fantasy Cricket?

Fantasy cricket is an online game where participants create virtual teams of real cricket players. Points are awarded based on the players' performances in real matches.

How do I Play Fantasy Cricket?

To play fantasy cricket, you need to register on a fantasy platform like EndgameEvent, select an upcoming match, create your team within the allotted budget, and earn points based on your players' on-field performances.

What are the Rules of Fantasy Cricket?

The rules of fantasy cricket typically involve selecting a balanced team within a budget, making strategic captain and vice-captain choices, and earning points for runs, wickets, catches, and other performances during the match.

Is Fantasy Cricket Legal?

Fantasy cricket is legal in many countries, including India, as it is considered a game of skill rather than chance. However, regulations may vary by jurisdiction, so it's important to check local laws and guidelines.
