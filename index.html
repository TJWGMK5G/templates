Question

What materials are your jewelry pieces made of?

Our jewelry pieces are crafted from high-quality materials such as sterling silver, 18k gold, and genuine gemstones, ensuring durability and lasting beauty.

Do you offer resizing services for rings?

Yes, we offer resizing services for rings to ensure the perfect fit. Our skilled technicians can adjust the size of your ring to your specifications for a comfortable and secure fit.

How can I care for my jewelry to maintain its brilliance?

We recommend cleaning your jewelry regularly with a soft cloth and mild soap to remove dirt and oils. Avoid exposing your jewelry to harsh chemicals and store it in a separate pouch or box to prevent scratches.

Can I request a custom design for a specific piece of jewelry?

Absolutely! We offer custom design services for customers looking to create a unique piece of jewelry. Our expert craftsmen will work closely with you to bring your vision to life, from concept to completion.
