Q: How do I create my fantasy cricket team on CricketHeroes?

A: Creating your team on CricketHeroes is easy! Simply sign up for an account, navigate to the team creation section, and select your preferred players based on their performance and availability. Once you've finalized your squad, you're ready to compete in leagues and tournaments.

Q: Can I change my team lineup after selecting it?

A: Yes, you can make changes to your team lineup before the start of each match. Use the substitution feature to swap players in and out based on their form, injuries, or other factors. Keep an eye on player updates and match schedules to optimize your team for maximum success.

Q: How are points calculated in CricketHeroes?

A: Points in CricketHeroes are awarded based on the performance of your selected players during real matches. Each action, such as runs scored, wickets taken, catches, and stumpings, earns points according to a predefined scoring system. Refer to the scoring guide on our platform for a detailed breakdown of points allocation.
