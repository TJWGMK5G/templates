Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

Q.

What types of pets do you cater to?

A.

We cater to a wide variety of pets, including dogs, cats, small mammals, birds, and reptiles. Whatever type of pet you have, we have products and services to meet their needs.

Q.

Do you offer grooming services for all breeds?

A.

Yes, our experienced groomers are trained to work with all breeds and sizes of dogs and cats. Whether your pet has long hair, short hair, or special grooming requirements, we can accommodate their needs.

Q.

How often should I bring my pet for grooming?

A.

The frequency of grooming depends on your pet's breed, coat type, and lifestyle. In general, we recommend scheduling grooming appointments every 4-6 weeks to keep your pet's coat clean and healthy.

Q.

Can I bring my pet's own food for daycare or boarding?

A.

Yes, you're welcome to bring your pet's own food for daycare or boarding. We want to ensure that your pet feels comfortable and maintains their regular diet while in our care.

Q.

Are your training programs suitable for older dogs?

A.

Absolutely! Our training programs are suitable for dogs of all ages, from puppies to seniors. It's never too late to teach your dog new tricks or improve their behavior.

Q.

How can I sign up for your home delivery subscription service?

A.

Signing up for our home delivery subscription service is easy! Simply visit our website or contact us directly to set up your subscription. Select your pet's favorite products, choose your delivery frequency, and enjoy the convenience of automatic deliveries to your doorstep.
