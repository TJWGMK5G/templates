Accessibility and inclusiveness:

Gyms should be accessible to everyone, regardless of fitness level, age or physical ability. They should offer a variety of programs and exercises so that everyone can find a suitable way to achieve their goals.

Safety and Health:

The top priority of gyms is to provide a safe and healthy fitness environment. This includes proper instruction on how to use the equipment, having qualified instructors and trainers, and maintaining standards of hygiene and cleanliness in the facilities.

Motivation and support:

Gyms need to inspire their visitors and provide them with support on the way to achieving their goals. This can include providing motivational workouts, creating a community of like-minded individuals for mutual support and motivation, and providing personal training and advice for optimal results.
