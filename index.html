Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here you’ll find answers to common questions about our language learning platform, courses, and services. We’re dedicated to making your experience smooth and rewarding. If you have further questions, feel free to reach out!

How do I choose the right course for my level?

We offer a placement test to assess your current language skills, ensuring you start with a course that matches your proficiency and goals.

Can I learn multiple languages at once?

Yes, our platform allows you to enroll in multiple courses, and you can manage your learning schedule for each language at your own pace.

What if I have a busy schedule?

No problem! Our flexible learning system lets you take lessons whenever it suits you, so you can fit language learning into your busy life.

Are the lessons live or pre-recorded?

We offer both live lessons with real-time interaction and pre-recorded sessions, allowing you to choose the format that works best for you.

Will I get personalized feedback?

Absolutely! Our instructors provide tailored feedback to help you improve, offering guidance based on your individual progress and learning needs.

Do I need any prior language experience to join?

No experience is required! We offer beginner courses for learners starting from scratch, as well as advanced classes for those looking to refine their skills.
