Frequently asked questions

Can I change my team during a tournament?

Yes, you can make changes to your team at any time before the start of each match in the tournament.

Where can I find more information about the rules and strategies of FantasyPitch?

You can find our rules and strategy guide on the "Help" page of our website. There, you'll find helpful tips and recommendations to improve your game.

How can I contact the FantasyPitch support team?

If you have any questions or issues related to playing on FantasyPitch, please send us a message through the feedback form on the website. Our support team will be happy to assist you!

Can I participate in more than one league at the same time?

Yes, you can join multiple leagues at the same time or create and manage your own leagues. Joining multiple leagues gives you more opportunities for competition and variety in the game.
