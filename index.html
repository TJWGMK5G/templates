Welcome to Chimes Aviation Academy

Celebrating 15 Years of Aviation Excellence

Chimes Aviation Academy (CAA) stands as India's premier Flying Training Organization, dedicated to delivering top-notch pilot training in South Asia. Endorsed by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) and partnered with leading airlines, we offer comprehensive pilot training for future aviators. With state-of-the-art facilities across multiple locations in Madhya Pradesh, your journey to becoming a skilled Chimes Aviator starts here.

Robust Alumni Network

CAA's close-knit community of graduates, thriving globally in the Aviation industry, reflects the unwavering quality and reliability of CAA's training programs.

State-of-the-Art Training Facilities

Featuring cutting-edge airport infrastructure and a modern fleet equipped with the latest avionics and safety systems, CAA ensures top-tier training and excellent value for investment.

Industry Acclaim

Our strong industry presence and unique Cadet Pilot Program distinguish us, offering numerous opportunities for our cadets and solidifying our reputation as a trusted leader in pilot training.

Why CAA?

The Top Choice for Pilot Training

A Comprehensive Flying Training Ecosystem with 3 Airbases.

CAA offers continuous flying opportunities with modern facilities, unrestricted airspace, and Madhya Pradesh's favorable year-round weather. The airbases are conveniently located just a cross-country flight apart, making CAA the ideal ecosystem for the best Commercial Pilot License (CPL) training in India.

A Career as a Pilot

Make the Sky Your Workplace

In a landscape of conventional careers, opt for a life where the sky is your office. Embrace a profession that goes beyond the ordinary, combining the excitement of flying with career advancement, all while being part of a vibrant community of dedicated aviators.

Life as a Cadet

Each day at CAA is an adventure, offering countless chances to forge lasting friendships with fellow Chimes Aviators. The dynamic student community encourages extracurricular activities and festive celebrations, creating an atmosphere of excitement and a true sense of belonging.

Train in India

Discover the Academy: A Comprehensive Flying Training Ecosystem

CAA’s advanced airbases in Madhya Pradesh are easily accessible via major highways and railways, with training sites just a cross-country flight apart. The on-campus hostels provide convenience and immerse cadets in airport operations around the clock.

Courses and Programs

Meeting All Your Flying Training Needs

Whether you’re pursuing the Cadet Pilot Program or exploring Hobby Flying, our tailored programs are designed to address the diverse training needs of every aviator.

CAA-IndiGo Cadet Pilot Program

CAA-IndiGo Cadet Pilot Program

Partnering with IndiGo, India’s leading airline, CAA offers a comprehensive and cost-effective program. This all-inclusive course covers both CPL and Type Rating, preparing you to step into a Junior First Officer role with IndiGo.

Commercial Pilot License

Commercial Pilot License (CPL IR-ME)

Experience the full journey from classroom learning to cockpit readiness. With Ground School training in Gurugram and flight training at Dhana and Neemuch airbases, we equip you with your CPL license and a pathway to a career in Commercial Aviation.

Chimes Flying Program (CFP)

Chimes Flying Program

Tailored for those who have completed their DGCA Ground School exams or are looking to transfer from another FTO, this program helps you complete your CPL training.

Testimonials

Voices of Proud Cadets, Alums, and Parents

Our students’ transformations from hopeful pilots to successful professionals highlight the excellence of the education and support at Chimes Aviation Academy. Explore their inspiring stories and find your own route to success with us.

The early morning training flights on the Cessna 172, the exhilaration of my first solo flight, and the milestone of landing the Diamond DA42 for my 200th hour are moments I’ll never forget. After graduating from CAA, I became a ground instructor and completed my Airbus A320 type rating at the Gulf Aviation Academy in Bahrain. I later established an aviation ground school with branches in Mumbai and Delhi, dedicated to offering comprehensive training for future pilots. Today, I’m proud to serve as a First Officer with Air India. Chimes Aviation Academy ignited a passion for aviation in me that extends far beyond flying.

Pallavi Mahato

First Officer

Amiksha Dewan
