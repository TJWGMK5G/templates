FAQ'S

Q: How do I create a team on StrikeFantasy?

A: To create a team, sign up or log in to your account, go to the "Create Team" section, and select your players within the given budget. Make sure to balance your team with a mix of batsmen, bowlers, all-rounders, and a wicket-keeper.

Q: How are points calculated?

A: Points are awarded based on players' real-life performances in matches. This includes runs scored, wickets taken, catches, and other key actions. Detailed points breakdown can be found in the "Scoring System" section on our website.

Q: Can I edit my team after submitting it?

A: Yes, you can edit your team before the match deadline. After the match starts, team changes are not allowed. Make sure to finalize your team well before the deadline.

Q: What are the different types of contests available?

A: We offer a variety of contests including Head-to-Head, Small Leagues, Mega Contests, and Practice Contests. Each contest type has different entry fees and prize structures to suit different preferences and skill levels.

Q: How do I withdraw my winnings?

A: To withdraw your winnings, go to the "My Account" section and select "Withdraw Funds." Enter the amount you wish to withdraw and choose your preferred withdrawal method. Follow the instructions to complete the process.

Q: What should I do if I encounter a problem?

A: If you experience any issues, our support team is here to help. Visit the "Contact Us" section and reach out via email or live chat. We aim to resolve all queries promptly to ensure you have a smooth experience on StrikeFantasy.
