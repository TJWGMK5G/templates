How to Play 10CRIC Cricket?

Playing fantasy cricket on 10CRIC is simple and fun. Follow these steps to get started:

    Sign Up / Log In:
  • Visit the 10CRIC website or download the app.
  • Sign up for a new account or log in if you already have one.
    Create Your Team:
  • Select a match or tournament you want to participate in.
  • Create your fantasy team by choosing real-life players from both teams. Stay within the budget limit and team composition rules.
    Join Contests:
  • Browse available contests and select one that suits you.
  • There are free contests and paid contests with varying entry fees and prize pools.
    Follow the Match:
  • Once the real-life match starts, your selected players will earn points based on their actual performance (e.g., runs scored, wickets taken, catches, etc.).
  • Track your team's performance in real-time and see where you rank against other participants.
    Earn Points and Win Prizes:
  • Points are awarded according to the performance of your selected players in the real match.
  • At the end of the match, prizes are distributed based on the contest's prize structure and your team’s ranking.
    Withdraw Your Winnings:
  • If you win, you can withdraw your prize money to your bank account or use it to enter more contests.

Frequently 10CRIC Asked Inquiries

How do I create an account on 10CRIC?

To create an account on 10CRIC, visit the website or download the app, click on the "Sign Up" button, and fill in the required details such as your name, email address, and password. Follow the instructions to complete the registration process.

How do I select players for my fantasy cricket team?

After logging in, choose a match or tournament you want to participate in. You'll have a budget to select players from both teams. Pick players based on their form, performance, and match conditions. Ensure you follow the team composition rules and budget limits.

What are the roles of Captain and Vice-Captain in my team?

The Captain and Vice-Captain are crucial as they earn extra points. The Captain earns double the points, and the Vice-Captain earns 1.5 times the points for their performances. Choose these roles wisely to maximize your team's score.

How do I join contests on 10CRIC?

Once you've created your team, browse the list of available contests. Select a contest that fits your preference, whether it's a free contest or one with an entry fee. Join the contest by confirming your participation and paying the entry fee if applicable.

How are points awarded to players?

Points are awarded based on the actual performance of the players in the real-life match. Points are given for runs scored, wickets taken, catches, and other cricketing actions. The detailed point system can be found on the 10CRIC website or app.

How can I withdraw my winnings from 10CRIC?

To withdraw your winnings, go to your account section and choose the withdrawal option. Follow the instructions to enter your bank details or preferred withdrawal method. The request will be processed, and funds will be transferred to your account.
