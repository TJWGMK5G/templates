Frequently asked questions

What are your gym's operating hours?

We are open 24/7, allowing you to work out at your convenience, day or night.

Can I try out the gym before committing to a membership?

Yes, we offer complimentary one-day passes for first-time visitors. Come experience our facilities firsthand!

Do you offer personal training services?

Absolutely! We have certified personal trainers available to help you reach your fitness goals. Please inquire at the front desk for more information.

Are there locker rooms and shower facilities available?

Yes, our gym is equipped with locker rooms, showers, and changing areas for your convenience.

What safety measures do you have in place to protect members during the COVID-19 pandemic?

We have implemented strict cleaning protocols, increased sanitization of equipment, and enforced social distancing measures throughout the gym. Additionally, we require all members to wear masks while inside the facility and have limited the number of people allowed in certain areas at one time.

Can I bring a guest with me to the gym?

Yes, we offer guest passes for a nominal fee. However, please check with our staff regarding current guest policies and availability.
