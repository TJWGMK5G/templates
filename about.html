Chimes Aviation Academy

For over 15 years, CAA has been dedicated to shaping the future of aviation, earning its place as one of India’s top Flying Training Organisations (FTOs). With operations at two bases—Dhana and Neemuch—and a new base opening soon in Shivpuri, the Academy is set to become South Asia's leading choice for flight training. Our partnerships with airlines for the Cadet Pilot Program further highlight our commitment to delivering exceptional training and maintaining our esteemed reputation.

About Us

Chimes Group

Chimes Group is a collection of diverse businesses guided by an independent and experienced management team. Each company within the Group aims to excel in its industry and deliver value to all stakeholders.

Petro IT

Petro IT is a leading provider of software products and services for the global Energy and Utility sectors. Established over a decade ago, the company merges deep domain expertise with innovative solutions, offering top-notch technology for effective asset management. With a global presence, Petro IT operates from offices in India, Ireland, the UAE, Indonesia, and the United States. The company boasts a prestigious client base and aims to forge new partnerships with outstanding organizations in the future.

Chimes Private Ltd

Chimes Private Limited handles Real Estate, external investments, collaborative projects, property developments, and more. As the primary investment vehicle of the Chimes Group, it aligns closely with the interests of the Group’s promoters. The company plays a crucial role in strategic decision-making and investment selections, including its major investment in Chimes Aviation Academy and Petro IT Limited. Chimes Private Limited oversees the Group's Real Estate Development projects.

Chimes Aviation Academy

Founded in Dhana, Madhya Pradesh, in 2008, Chimes Aviation Academy is a premier Flying Training Organisation approved by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). Over its 15-year history, CAA has trained more than 400 pilots from India and abroad. The Academy has expanded to three bases—Dhana, Neemuch, and Shivpuri—and underscores its commitment to high-quality aviation training through its prestigious Cadet Pilot Program, all supported by the Chimes Group.
