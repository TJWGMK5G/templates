What Is Your Policy On Pets?

At OfficeHive, we understand that pets are part of the family, and we strive to create a welcoming environment for all tenants, including those with furry friends. Our pet policy allows well-behaved pets in designated areas of our office spaces, subject to approval and compliance with certain guidelines. We require tenants to register their pets with our management team and provide documentation of vaccinations and licensing where applicable. Additionally, tenants are responsible for ensuring that their pets do not disrupt the work environment or cause any damage to the property. By fostering a pet-friendly atmosphere, we aim to accommodate the diverse needs of our tenants while maintaining a professional and harmonious workplace. Contact us for more information on our pet policy and how we can accommodate your furry companions in our office spaces.
