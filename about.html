Our Journey

Maverick Aviation was established by experienced pilots dedicated to the industry. With years of hands-on experience navigating its challenges, we meticulously crafted study programs to help aspiring pilots overcome all obstacles and achieve their Commercial Pilot License.

Our Vision is to offer high-quality, affordable, and authentic professional pilot training programs.
Our Mission is to inspire you to explore new possibilities and empower you to live your most extraordinary life while mastering professional airmanship.
Why Choose Us?
At Maverick Aviation, we’ve developed a unique approach with interactive teaching methods that make mastering complex concepts and numerical problems effortless.
Our training Begins with the fundamentals and advances to the most intricate topics as the course progresses.
You’ll receive lifetime access to The Maverick Vault — our exclusive online library featuring over 150 handpicked books, flight trainers, Q-banks, and CBT programs.
Additionally, enjoy lifetime access to The Maverick Academy Server, where you can post questions, access daily classwork, get updates on job openings, and engage in live chats with mentors, airline pilots, and industry experts.
Why Do We Achieve Consistent Results?
Structured Study Schedule At Maverick Aviation, our well-organized study schedule allows students to prepare efficiently and comfortably, reducing stress while maintaining effectiveness.
Weekly Topic-Based Tests These tests build confidence and improve time management skills, crucial for performing well in exams.
Extensive Workbooks and Q-Banks Throughout the course, students tackle over 4,000-5,000 questions, ensuring comprehensive preparation for all aspects of the DGCA CPL exams.
Meet the Team
Capt Shriyansh Talekar
As one of the founding members of our institute, Capt Shriyansh brings a wealth of experience to Maverick Aviation. He holds a CPL and a BSc in Aviation from the University of Mumbai, where he also serves as a paper setter and moderator. With certification as a DGCA Ground and Synthetic Flight Instructor and extensive experience with top flying clubs, he brings valuable insights into commercial flying and training. Rated on the Airbus A320, Capt Shriyansh uses his extensive industry knowledge to inspire and guide aspiring pilots. He also shares his expertise on his YouTube channel, where he’s known as Captain Maverick. At Maverick Aviation, he specializes in Air Navigation, using humor to make learning engaging and effective, helping students grasp concepts with ease.
Capt Jayesh Kirtikar
Capt Jayesh Kirtikar completed his CPL training in India and holds a degree in Aviation from Mumbai University. As a Ground Instructor at several prestigious institutes and an Examiner and Moderator for Mumbai University, he brings a wealth of experience to his role. A co-founder of Maverick Aviation, Capt Jayesh teaches subjects including Meteorology, Technical and Regulations, and RTR. His extensive background in ground training ensures that students benefit from some of the most knowledgeable and experienced faculty in the aviation industry. He plays a crucial role in enhancing the quality of education at Maverick Aviation. Specializing in accommodating time-constrained students, including working professionals, Capt Jayesh designs personalized schedules and assignments to fit their needs. His deep expertise and tailored approach significantly aid in preparing students for the DGCA exams.
Capt Dharav Modi
Capt Dharav Modi holds a B.Tech in Instrumentation & Control Engineering and earned his CPL in 2020 from a renowned flying school. He is rated on the A320 family of aircraft and has additional certifications in Air Law and Air Navigation from IATA. At Maverick Aviation, Capt Dharav specializes in Air Regulations, Aviation Meteorology, and RTR(A), and he also teaches BSc Aviation courses at Mumbai University. His deep technical expertise and distinctive teaching methods make complex theoretical concepts more accessible to students. Additionally, Capt Dharav serves as a counselor at Maverick Aviation, where his approachable and supportive demeanor helps address students' initial questions and concerns effectively.
Capt Mrugank Talekar
Capt Mrugank Talekar, a recent graduate with a CPL and a BSc in Aviation from Mumbai University (2022), is Maverick Aviation's young prodigy. Despite completing his training at just 21, he brings a wealth of practical and theoretical knowledge to his role. He teaches Air Navigation, Meteorology, and RTR, always ensuring no student is left behind. Capt Mrugank is known for his encouragement, guidance, and constructive feedback, creating a friendly and engaging learning environment. At Maverick, he embodies the spirit of making learning enjoyable, earning him the nickname "ICEMAN."
Capt Shelly Pinto
Capt Shelly Pinto completed her CPL training in India and holds a degree in Aviation from Mumbai University. At Maverick Aviation, she specializes in teaching Aviation Meteorology and is passionate about sharing her expertise with aspiring pilots.
