We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund.

Do you offer free shipping?

We offer free shipping on orders over $50. For orders under $50, there is a flat shipping rate of $5.

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-2 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been shipped.
