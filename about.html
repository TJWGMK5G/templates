Questions

Frequently Asked Question

How do I determine my ring size?

We offer a complimentary ring sizing guide that you can use at home or visit our store for a professional sizing. Alternatively, you can also use an existing ring that fits you well and measure its inner diameter to determine your size.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your location. Please contact our customer service team for more information.

What materials are your jewelry pieces made of?

Our jewelry pieces are crafted from high-quality materials such as sterling silver, 18k gold, and genuine gemstones, ensuring durability and lasting beauty.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free return policy within 30 days of purchase for unworn and undamaged items. Simply contact our customer service team to initiate the return process and receive a full refund or exchange.
