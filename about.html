FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What instruments do you offer lessons for?

We offer lessons for a wide range of instruments, including piano, guitar, violin, drums, flute, saxophone, and more.

2. Do I need any prior experience to enroll?

No prior experience is necessary! We welcome students of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced musicians.

3. How long are the lessons?

Our lessons typically range from 30 minutes to one hour, depending on the student's preference and instructor availability.

4. Can I choose my own lesson schedule?

Yes, you can! We offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate your busy lifestyle.

5. Are online lessons as effective as in-person lessons?

Absolutely! Our online lessons are conducted by experienced instructors using advanced technology, ensuring a high-quality learning experience from the comfort of your home.

6. Do you provide instruments for lessons?

While we recommend having your own instrument for practice, we can provide instruments for in-person lessons upon request.

7. What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that life can be unpredictable. We offer a flexible cancellation policy with advance notice.

8. How can I track my progress?

Your instructor will provide regular feedback and assessments to track your progress. Additionally, we offer performance opportunities and recitals for students to showcase their skills.
