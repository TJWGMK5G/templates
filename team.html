Frequently Asked Questions: Your Guide to Dazzle & Shine

Welcome to our FAQ section! At Dazzle & Shine, we understand that choosing the perfect jewelry piece or navigating our services can raise questions. That's why we've compiled a comprehensive list of frequently asked questions to provide you with the information you need. Whether you're curious about our design process, care tips for your jewelry, or shipping and return policies, our goal is to ensure that you feel informed and confident in your decisions.

What materials do you use in your jewelry?

We take pride in using only high-quality materials, including ethically sourced gold, silver, and a variety of precious and semi-precious gemstones. Each piece is crafted to ensure longevity and beauty, allowing you to enjoy your jewelry for years to come.

Can I customize a piece of jewelry?

Absolutely! We offer a range of customization options, from selecting different gemstones to designing unique settings. Our skilled designers will work closely with you to create a piece that perfectly reflects your vision.

How should I care for my jewelry?

To keep your jewelry looking its best, we recommend storing it in a soft pouch or a jewelry box to prevent scratches. Clean your pieces regularly with a gentle cloth and avoid exposure to harsh chemicals or prolonged sunlight.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your jewelry, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange, provided it is in its original condition and packaging.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping for all purchases! Just let us know at checkout, and we’ll ensure your gift is beautifully presented and ready to delight your loved one.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order's status online until it arrives at your doorstep.

Do you provide international shipping?

Yes, we proudly offer international shipping to select countries! During checkout, you can choose your destination to see shipping options and fees available for your location.

What if my jewelry needs repair?

We offer repair services for our jewelry to ensure that it remains in excellent condition. If your piece requires maintenance or repair, please contact our customer service team, and they will guide you through the process.
