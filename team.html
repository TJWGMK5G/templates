Customers Frequently Ask: Your Questions, Answered at Botanical Bliss

How do I care for my new plants?

To ensure your new plants thrive, start by placing them in a location with appropriate light conditions. Each plant has specific light requirements, so check the care tag. Water the plant when the top inch of soil feels dry, and use a pot with drainage holes to prevent overwatering. Additionally, feed your plants with a balanced fertilizer during the growing season for optimal health.

What should I do if my plant starts to wilt?

Wilting can be a sign of various issues, such as underwatering, overwatering, or inadequate light. First, check the soil moisture—if it's too dry, give your plant a good drink. If the soil is soggy, allow it to dry out before watering again. Assess the lighting conditions and move the plant to a brighter spot if necessary. If problems persist, inspect for pests or diseases and take appropriate action.
