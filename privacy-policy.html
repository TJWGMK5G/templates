Privacy Policy

By using VirtuosoVibes, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal information as described herein. We also collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and device identifiers. We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. These technologies help us understand how you interact with our site and improve our services. You may choose to provide additional information voluntarily, such as your musical interests or feedback on our courses. Providing this information is optional but helps us tailor our services to better meet your needs. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

Your personal information enables us to process transactions, provide customer support, and communicate with you about our services, promotions, and updates.
