Privacy-Policy

Data collected from cookies and similar technologies helps us improve our website's functionality, security, and performance. In certain circumstances, we may also disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our policies, or protect our rights and interests. Rest assured that we take reasonable steps to ensure that any third parties with whom we share your information adhere to strict privacy and security standards.
  • Data Retention. We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. If you no longer wish to have your information retained, you may request deletion of your account or contact us to remove specific data. However, please note that certain information may need to be retained for legal or regulatory compliance purposes, such as tax or accounting requirements. We will securely dispose of or anonymize your data once it is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected. Rest assured that we take appropriate measures to protect your information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Our response includes conducting thorough investigations, identifying the scope of the breach, and taking appropriate steps to prevent future incidents. We prioritize transparency and communication throughout the process to ensure our customers are informed and supported. This policy ensures that we only retain data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in our privacy policy or to comply with legal obligations.
