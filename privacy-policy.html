Privacy Policy

    Collection of Personal Information: FantasyWicket collects personal information such as name, email address, and location during the registration process. This information is used to create and manage user accounts and to personalize the gaming experience. Additionally, we may collect device information, IP addresses, and browser data for security and analytical purposes.

    Use of Personal Information: The personal information collected by FantasyWicket is used to provide and improve our services, including facilitating gameplay, processing transactions, and delivering targeted advertisements. We may also use this information to communicate with users, provide customer support, and conduct research and analysis to enhance our platform.

  • Data Security Measures: FantasyWicket implements industry-standard security measures to protect the personal information of our users. This includes encryption protocols, firewalls, and regular security audits to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of user data. However, it is important to note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
  • Third-Party Services: FantasyWicket may utilize third-party services, such as analytics providers and advertising partners, to enhance our platform and deliver personalized content to users. These third parties may have access to certain user information, but they are contractually obligated to use it only for authorized purposes and to maintain its confidentiality.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: FantasyWicket uses cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about user interactions with our platform. These technologies enable us to personalize the user experience, track usage patterns, and gather demographic information for targeted advertising purposes. Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.
  • Children's Privacy: FantasyWicket is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it as soon as possible.
  • Data Retention: FantasyWicket retains personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. User accounts may be deactivated upon request, and certain data may be retained for archival, audit, or legal purposes.
  • Opt-Out Options: Users have the option to opt out of certain data collection and processing activities, such as targeted advertising, by adjusting their account settings or contacting customer support. However, please note that opting out may limit certain features and functionalities of the platform. FantasyWicket@gmail.com.

  • California Privacy Rights: California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to request disclosure of personal information collected by FantasyWicket, the right to opt out of the sale of personal information, and the right to request deletion of personal information.
  • European Union Data Subjects: European Union data subjects have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, or erase personal data, the right to restrict or object to processing, and the right to data portability.
  • Contact Information: For questions, concerns, or requests related to this privacy policy or the handling of personal information by FantasyWicket, users can contact our privacy team
  • Consent to Privacy Policy: By using FantasyWicket, users consent to the collection, processing, and storage of their personal information as described in this privacy policy.   • Data Subject Requests: FantasyWicket will respond to data subject requests, including requests to exercise user rights, within the timeframes and in accordance with the requirements of applicable data protection laws.
  • Data Breach Notification: In the event of a data breach involving personal information, FantasyWicket will notify affected users as required by law and take appropriate measures to mitigate any potential harm.
