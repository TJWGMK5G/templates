Privacy policy

    At VirtuosoVerse, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you visit our online music school. We value the trust you place in us and are dedicated to protecting your personal data. By using our website and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, you may choose not to use our services.

    We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. Personal identification information, such as your name, email address, and contact details, is collected when you register for our courses. We also gather non-personal identification information, including your browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps. This information helps us analyze trends and enhance the user experience on our website. We may also use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your interaction with our site.

  • The information we collect serves multiple purposes aimed at enhancing your experience with VirtuosoVerse. We use your personal data to provide and manage your account, process payments, and deliver course content. Your information may also be used to communicate with you about updates, promotions, and important changes to our services. Additionally, we analyze user data to improve our offerings and tailor the learning experience to meet your needs. We are dedicated to ensuring that your information is used only for legitimate purposes and in accordance with applicable laws.
  • We value your privacy and do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering our services. These third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are not allowed to use it for any other purpose. We may also disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities. Your trust is essential to us, and we take all necessary precautions to ensure your data remains protected.
  • Your user account is protected by a password that you create. It is your responsibility to maintain the confidentiality of your password and account information. You should notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized use of your account or if your password is compromised. We will not be liable for any loss or damage arising from your failure to protect your password or account information. To help ensure the security of your account, we recommend that you use a strong password and change it regularly.
  • With your consent, we may send you promotional materials and information about our services that may be of interest to you. You have the right to opt-out of receiving these communications at any time. You can do this by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the emails we send or by contacting us directly. Your preferences regarding marketing communications can be updated in your account settings. We are committed to respecting your communication preferences and will honor your choices regarding marketing messages.
  • We process your personal data based on various legal grounds, including your consent, the necessity of processing for the performance of a contract, and compliance with our legal obligations. By using our services, you consent to the collection and processing of your data as described in this Privacy Policy. If we rely on your consent to process your personal data, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. We will ensure that any processing of your data complies with applicable laws and regulations.
  • As a user of VirtuosoVerse, you have rights regarding your personal data under applicable data protection laws. These rights may include the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of your data, as well as the right to data portability and the right to object to processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us, and we will assist you in addressing your request. We are committed to protecting your rights and ensuring that your personal data is handled in accordance with the law. contact@VirtuosoVerse.com
