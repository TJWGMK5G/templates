Privacy policy

    We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes personal information that you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you create an account, place an order, or contact customer service. Additionally, we gather data automatically, like your IP address, browser type, and access times when you visit our website. This information helps us understand user behavior and improve our services.

    Our collection methods include online forms, cookies, and web analytics tools. Cookies are small files stored on your device that enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and login information. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our site.

  • In certain cases, we may receive information about you from third parties, such as social media platforms if you choose to connect your account. This could include your profile information, friend lists, and other data that you have allowed those platforms to share. We will treat any such data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
  • We also use your information to communicate with you effectively. This may involve sending you promotional materials, newsletters, and updates about your orders. You can opt out of receiving promotional communications at any time by following the unsubscribe link in our emails. In some instances, your information may be used for research and analysis to better understand our customers' needs and improve our offerings. By utilizing this data, we aim to enhance your shopping experience and provide more personalized content that meets your interests.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted partners to facilitate our services. These partners may include payment processors, shipping companies, and marketing platforms that help us operate our business. We ensure that any third-party service providers comply with strict confidentiality and security standards.
  • In certain situations, we may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights. This includes cooperating with law enforcement or responding to legal requests. We may also share information in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets.
  • We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services we use. This will help you understand how they handle your information and what choices you may have regarding your data. We are committed to ensuring that any third parties we work with uphold similar privacy standards as those outlined in our policy.
  • You have certain rights regarding your personal information, and we are committed to helping you exercise those rights. This includes the right to access the information we hold about you, request corrections, or delete your data under specific circumstances. To make such requests, please contact our customer service team, and we will assist you promptly. contact@TimelessFurnish.com
  • You can also choose to opt out of marketing communications at any time. If you no longer wish to receive promotional emails, simply click the "unsubscribe" link provided in the email or contact us to be removed from our mailing list. However, please note that you may still receive essential account-related information.
  • Additionally, you have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data under certain conditions. We will evaluate your request and respond accordingly, ensuring that your preferences are respected while complying with applicable laws.
  • Our website may contain links to third-party websites that are not operated by TimelessFurnish. We have no control over and are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of these external sites. Therefore, we encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.
  • When you click on a link to a third-party site, you may be subject to that site's privacy policy and practices. TimelessFurnish does not endorse or make any representations about these websites, their content, or their privacy policies. Your interactions with these websites are at your own risk. We strive to provide valuable content and resources, but we cannot guarantee the accuracy or reliability of information found on third-party websites. Always exercise caution and make informed decisions when navigating the internet.
