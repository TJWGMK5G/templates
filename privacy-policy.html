Privacy Policy

    At AnimalAlley, we prioritize the privacy of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect from our website users. It also explains how we use and protect this information. By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

    AnimalAlley collects various types of information to provide better services to our customers. This includes personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number. We may also collect non-personal information like your browsing behavior on our site. This data helps us understand user preferences and improve our offerings. We collect information through forms, cookies, and other tracking technologies.

  • The information we collect is used to enhance your shopping experience. Personal information is primarily used to process orders and communicate with you. Non-personal information helps us analyze trends and improve our website. We may also use your information to send promotional emails about new products or special offers. Rest assured, we do not sell or lease your personal information to third parties.
  • AnimalAlley implements strict security measures to protect your information. We use encryption technologies to safeguard your data during transmission. Additionally, our servers are secured to prevent unauthorized access. We regularly review our security policies to ensure the highest level of protection. Despite our best efforts, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure.
  • Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences. They enable us to provide personalized content and improve site functionality. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.
  • AnimalAlley may use third-party services to enhance our website and services. These third parties may have access to your information as necessary to perform their functions. However, they are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the intended purposes. We do not share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Any third-party links on our site are subject to their own privacy policies.
  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by us. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us through the provided channels. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe. In some cases, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request. We aim to ensure that your information is accurate and up-to-date.
  • AnimalAlley does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us immediately. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children online. Our website is not intended for use by children without parental supervision. AnimalAlley@gmail.com.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information. Your continued use of our website after any changes indicates your acceptance of the new terms. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our site.
  • We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. This includes maintaining your account, processing transactions, and complying with legal obligations. When your information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. Our data retention practices ensure that we manage your information responsibly. If you have any questions about our data retention policies, please contact us.

You can opt-out of receiving these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails. If you wish to deactivate your account, please contact our customer support team. We will process your request promptly and ensure your data is handled according to this Privacy Policy. Your account security is our priority, and we recommend using strong passwords.

All payment information is encrypted and transmitted securely. If you have any concerns about payment security, please contact us. We are committed to providing a safe and secure shopping experience. AnimalAlley@gmail.com.
  • We comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding data privacy and protection. This includes adhering to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for our European customers. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data security. If you believe we have violated any legal requirements, please contact us immediately. We will investigate and take appropriate action to resolve any issues.
