Privacy Policy

    Information Collection: BloomBotanica collects personal information such as name, email address, and phone number when customers make a purchase or sign up for our newsletter.

  • Opt-Out Policy: Customers can opt out of receiving promotional emails or newsletters by following the instructions provided in each communication.

  • Changes to Policy: BloomBotanica reserves the right to update or change its privacy policy at any time. Any modifications will be effective immediately upon posting on the website.

  • Compliance: We are committed to complying with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for customers in the European Union.
  • Effective Date: This privacy policy is effective as and applies to all users of the BloomBotanica website.
