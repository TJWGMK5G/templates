Privacy-Policy

    Information Collection LanguageLoom collects personal information from users solely for the purpose of providing language learning services. This information may include name, email address, and demographic details. Users are informed about the types of data collected and how it will be used before submitting any personal information.

    Data Usage Personal information collected by LanguageLoom is used to personalize the learning experience, track progress, and communicate with users about course updates and relevant offers. LanguageLoom ensures that user data is securely stored and not shared with third parties without explicit consent.

  • Cookies Policy LanguageLoom uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. Cookies are small files stored on the user's device that track browsing behavior. Users have the option to accept or decline cookies, although declining may affect certain functionalities of the website.
  • Security Measures LanguageLoom employs industry-standard security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption, firewalls, and regular security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
  • Third-Party Links LanguageLoom may contain links to third-party websites or services for additional learning resources. Users should review the privacy policies of these third parties as LanguageLoom is not responsible for their practices or policies regarding data collection and privacy.
  • User Consent By using LanguageLoom's services, users consent to the collection and use of their personal information as outlined in this privacy policy. Users have the right to withdraw consent or request deletion of their data at any time.
  • Data Retention LanguageLoom retains user data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy or as required by law. Users can request the deletion of their data after completing their language courses.
  • Children's Privacy LanguageLoom is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information should contact us to have it removed.
  • Updates to Privacy Policy LanguageLoom may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or regulatory requirements. Users will be notified of any significant changes, and their continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
  • Data Access and Control Users have the right to access, update, or delete their personal information stored by LanguageLoom. Requests for data access or modifications can be made through the user's account settings or by contacting LanguageLoom's support team.
  • International Data Transfers LanguageLoom may transfer user data to servers located outside the user's country for processing and storage. By using LanguageLoom's services, users consent to the transfer of their data to these locations, which may have different data protection laws.
  • Marketing Communications LanguageLoom may send marketing communications to users who have opted in to receive them. Users can unsubscribe from these communications at any time by following the instructions provided in the emails or contacting LanguageLoom's support team.
  • Data Accuracy LanguageLoom takes reasonable steps to ensure that the personal information collected is accurate, complete, and up to date. Users can review and update their information through their account settings.
  • Data Portability Upon request, LanguageLoom will provide users with a copy of their personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. This allows users to transfer their data to another service if they choose.
  • User Feedback LanguageLoom welcomes feedback from users regarding their experiences with the platform and any concerns about privacy or data security. Users can contact LanguageLoom's support team to submit feedback or report issues. LanguageLoom@gmail.com.
  • Compliance with Laws LanguageLoom complies with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union. Users have rights under these laws regarding the collection, use, and retention of their personal information.
  • Contact Information Users can contact LanguageLoom's support team for any questions, concerns, or requests related to privacy and data protection. Contact information is provided on the LanguageLoom website.
  • Data Breach Notification In the event of a data breach involving user information, LanguageLoom will notify affected users promptly and take appropriate measures to mitigate any potential harm. Users will be informed of the nature of the breach and steps they can take to protect themselves.
  • Confidentiality LanguageLoom respects the confidentiality of user data and ensures that only authorized personnel have access to it. All employees undergo training on data protection practices and are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Governing Law This privacy policy and any disputes arising from it are governed by the laws of the jurisdiction where LanguageLoom is headquartered. Users agree to submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in that jurisdiction for resolving any disputes related to this policy.
