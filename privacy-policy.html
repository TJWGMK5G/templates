Privacy policy

    Information Collection: At SparkleShine, we collect personal information from our customers to provide them with a seamless shopping experience. This information may include names, addresses, contact details, and payment information. We ensure that all data collected is done so in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

    Use of Information: The information we collect is used to process orders, fulfill requests, and provide personalized services to our customers. Additionally, we may use this information to communicate with customers about their orders, promotions, and updates related to our products and services.

  • Data Security: We take the security of our customers' data seriously and have implemented measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We may share information with trusted partners who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business.

Cookies are small files stored on users' devices that track their interactions with the site, such as pages visited and items added to cart. Users can adjust their browser settings to disable cookies, although this may affect their ability to access certain features of the site.
  • Opt-Out Policy: Customers have the option to opt out of receiving promotional communications from SparkleShine at any time. They can do so by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the communication or by contacting us directly.
