Privacy Policy

    AuraDesign respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or engage with our services. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information. By accessing our website or using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy. Our privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this policy regularly for any updates. This policy applies to all visitors, users, and customers who interact with AuraDesign's website or services.

    We use the information we collect to provide, operate, and improve our services. This includes communicating with you, processing transactions, and fulfilling your requests for information or services. We may use your information to personalize your experience on our website, such as recommending products or services that may be of interest to you based on your browsing history or preferences. Your information helps us understand trends and preferences of our users, allowing us to enhance our website, services, and marketing strategies. We may use your email address to send you newsletters, updates, promotional materials, and other communications that you have opted-in to receive. You can opt-out of receiving these communications at any time by following the instructions provided in the emails or contacting us directly. AuraDesign does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

  • AuraDesign employs industry-standard security measures to protect the confidentiality and integrity of your personal information. We use encryption, firewalls, and secure socket layer technology (SSL) to safeguard your data during transmission. Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel who need to know the information to perform their job duties. We regularly review and update our security practices to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. Despite our efforts to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. It is important to keep your account credentials confidential and to log out of your account when using shared or public computers to prevent unauthorized access to your information. If you believe that your personal information has been compromised or if you have any concerns about the security of your data, please contact us immediately.
  • AuraDesign may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to you. These service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only for the purposes of providing services to us and are prohibited from disclosing or using your information for any other purpose. We may disclose your information in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements. In the event that AuraDesign undergoes a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your information. We may also share aggregated or anonymized information that does not personally identify you with third parties for marketing, advertising, research, or other purposes. You have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information held by AuraDesign. You can do this by contacting us using the information provided at the end of this policy.
  • AuraDesign retains your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you wish to request the deletion of your personal information or if you no longer wish to use our services, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy. Even if we delete your personal information, it may remain in backup or archival copies for legal, tax, or regulatory purposes. AuraDesign will securely destroy or anonymize your personal information once it is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • You have certain rights regarding your personal information under applicable data protection laws. These rights may include the right to access, correct, update, restrict processing, object to processing, and delete your personal information. You may also have the right to receive a copy of your personal information in a commonly used electronic format to transfer it to another company (data portability). If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law. Please note that we may require additional information from you to verify your identity before fulfilling your request. You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe that we have violated your rights or failed to comply with applicable data protection laws.
