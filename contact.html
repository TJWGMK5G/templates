What services does Celebration Station offer?

Celebration Station provides a comprehensive range of event planning services, including wedding planning, corporate event management, themed parties, charity and fundraising events, milestone celebrations, and full-service event coordination. Our packages are designed to cater to various needs and ensure a memorable experience for every occasion.

How can I book an event with Celebration Station?

To book an event with us, simply reach out through our contact form or call our office directly. We’ll schedule a consultation to discuss your event details, preferences, and requirements. Once we have all the necessary information, we’ll prepare a proposal and work with you to finalize the details and secure your date.

Can I customize my event package?

Absolutely! Our event packages are fully customizable to suit your specific needs and preferences. During our consultation, we’ll discuss your vision and make recommendations for adjustments or additions to ensure the package aligns perfectly with your event goals and personal style.

What is included in the Luxe Wedding Package?

The Luxe Wedding Package includes comprehensive services such as venue selection, custom floral design, décor, catering, bar service, live entertainment, and a dedicated wedding day coordinator. This all-inclusive package ensures that every aspect of your wedding is handled with precision and elegance.
